About Queck

While half of the world population is in quarantine, it's become more important than ever to have a thermometer in your house. One of the main symptoms of the coronavirus is a high fever, so you'll want to monitor body temperature if you or someone you care for starts to feel ill. All temperature data is automatically stored into the and shared online in your Quarantine profile. Alternatively you can share with your friends and family as well.

About Queck

Check my Quarantine (Queck) was build by Marius Dima and submited as part of COVID-19 Global Hackathon in 30th March 2020.

The COVID-19 Global Hackathon is an opportunity for developers to build software solutions that drive social impact, with the aim of tackling some of the challenges related to the current coronavirus (COVID-19) pandemic.
